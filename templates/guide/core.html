Getting Started

Logging in

Use your username and password on the login screen. Contact your admin if you haven't received credentials yet.

Forgot password?

Click "Forgot password?" on the login page — a reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

Dashboard

After login you land on the Dashboard. It shows leads, quotations, and recent activity — automatically scoped to your team.

Your role

Member, Lead, or Admin — your role controls what you can see and do. Admins see everything; Leads approve quotations; Members create and manage records.

Dashboard → My Profile →

Your Profile

What you can update

Your display name, phone number, and branch. These appear in the sidebar footer and on records you create.

What only admins can change

Your username, role, and team assignment. If any of these are wrong, ask your admin to correct them.

Changing your password

Use the "Change password" link on your profile page. You'll need to enter your current password first.

Profile →

Notifications

Red badge in sidebar

That's your unread count. Click Notifications in the sidebar to see them all.

When you're notified

New lead assigned, quotation approved, quotation won/lost, market order confirmed, new case created, or a new email inquiry received for your team.

Mark all as read

Use the "Mark all read" button on the Notifications page to clear the badge in one click.

Notifications →

Team Chat

Channels

You see All Staff plus your own team's channel. Admins can see all team channels.

Sending messages

Press Enter to send. Press Shift + Enter to add a new line within your message.

Auto-refresh

Chat polls every 4 seconds — new messages appear automatically without refreshing the page.

Message history

The last 100 messages per channel are visible. Older messages are not retained in the UI.

Admin — User Management

Adding a user

Go to Add User in the sidebar. Select the team first — the role dropdown filters to only valid roles for that team automatically.

Editing roles

From the Users directory, click a user's row → Edit. Role and permission changes take effect immediately — no logout required.

Approving new accounts

Self-registered users are inactive by default. Activate them from the Users directory or the Admin Panel → Users.

Admin Panel

The Django Admin at /admin/ gives full raw database access — ProductKeywords, TeamEmailConfig, and all model records. Use carefully.

Users → Add User → Admin Panel →

Core & CRM Quotation Automator Training

Working with Leads

Creating a lead

Click "New Lead" from the Leads list. Fill in the customer's name, company, and contact. Source indicates how the inquiry arrived.

Customer auto-link

When a quotation is saved, the customer record is automatically created or updated from the lead — no separate entry needed.

Broker-sourced leads

Attach a broker in the lead form if the inquiry came through one. Broker leads use an internal quotation format and send rate-only emails.

Case notes & voice dictation

Open a lead → Case Notes card. Dictate with the microphone button (Chrome/Edge only), clean up with AI, then convert to a formal Case.

Leads → New Lead →

Quotation Editor

Starting a quotation

From a lead's detail page, click "New Quotation". The AI attempts to pre-fill line items from the lead text — review everything before saving.

The product picker

Click ⌕ pick on any row to open the product catalogue. This sets the product, make, length, HSN code, and purchase rate automatically. Always use the picker — don't type manually.

Locked fields

Make and Length are set only via the picker — they're read-only otherwise. Pcs is greyed for products with no piece count.

Auto-calculation

Unit price, totals, taxes, and grand total all update live in the browser as you type. The final values are what gets saved.

Quotations →

Pricing Add-ons

Opening add-ons

Click ⊞ add-ons on any line item row to expand 7 per-row cost inputs: Parity, Cutting, Loading, Transport, Margin, Interest, Commission.

Defaults from customer notes

If the customer record has pricing defaults saved, those values load automatically when you open the quotation editor.

Add-ons are not saved

These inputs live only in your browser session. Only the final unit price is stored in the database — if you reload, re-enter them.

Discount per row

Each line item has a Discount % field. The discounted total (final price) is used for tax and grand total calculations.

Approving & Sending

Who can approve

Only Team Leads and Admins can approve a quotation. Members create and edit drafts, then request approval.

Sending to customer

After approval, click "Send". A confirmation screen lets you review the email and PDF before it goes out.

PDF attachment

PDF is generated automatically and attached to the customer email. Broker leads get a text-only rate email — no PDF sent externally.

Status flow
Draft→ Approved→ Sent

Revisions & Outcomes

Revising a quotation

Click "Revise" on a sent quotation to create a new version (v2, v3…). The original is preserved. All versions are linked to the same lead.

Shared outcome

Win/Loss/Not Updated applies to the entire deal — all versions share it. You pick which specific version closed the deal when marking as Won.

Marking as Won

Stock is deducted exactly once on the first Win. If you change outcome and mark Won again, stock is not deducted a second time.

Stock deduction is irreversible

Changing outcome to Loss or Not Updated after a Win does not restore stock — the physical goods have already moved.

Market Orders

What is a Market Order?

Tracks the logistics of a broker-sourced deal — from rate negotiation through to delivery order issuance. Separate from the Quotation flow.

Status pipeline
New→ Rate Sent→ Confirmed→ DO Pending→ Completed
Loading dock assignment

Team Leads assign a loading dock member after broker confirmation. That member receives an email notification automatically.

Delivery Order (DO)

Enter the DO number on the order detail page to complete the order and send the DO confirmation to the broker's email.

Lead / Admin — Email Inbox Polling

What polling does

Checks each team's inbox for new inquiries. Valid emails become Leads automatically. Broker replies update the relevant Market Order status.

Manual poll

Click "Poll Inbox" on the Leads list page for an immediate check — useful when waiting for a specific reply to come through.

Automatic schedule

The system polls automatically every 30 minutes via a background cron job. No manual trigger needed in normal operation.

Spam filter

Emails are pre-filtered before hitting the AI. Marketing mailers, delivery receipts, and non-inquiries are discarded silently.
